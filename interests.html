I have played many, many games since my childhood and many of them have become major parts of who I am. Although I hope to work in the video game industry, I am very passionate about all techonology in general. My belief is that techonology exists to enhance the lives of the people that use it.

Extracurricular Activities

Event Administrator, Nevada eSports, UNR

My duty as an Event Admin was to ensure that our video game tournaments, which were held twice a semester, ran smoothly. These tournaments have around 120 participants along with 1000+ spectators (including live spectators and viewers of online stream). At the tournament, I would be doing tasks including: managing the player room to ensure games started punctually, troubleshoot problems that occur with peripherals during a match, operating the check-in desk and taking registration fees, and assisting with setup and takedown of the venue.

Family Head, Nevada Circle K, UNR

Circle K International is the most widely known collegiate service organization. They are a part of the Kiwanis organization, which includes Key Club in high school. I was a general member for my first two years in the club and went to various service and social events. As a Family Head in my third year with the club, I was assigned a "family" within the club. This family included 10 other members of the club. My duty as a family head was to encourage my "children" to participate in more events with the club and to foster friendships with them all. Although I was not the most active member of the club, I got to know many of my family members relatively well. A couple of them even gave me the nickname "Legen-daddy," since our family was the Legen-wait for it-dary family based on the tv show How I Met Your Mother.
